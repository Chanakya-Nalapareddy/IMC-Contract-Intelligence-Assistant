 THIS CONSTRUCTION MANAGER’S  CONSENT AND ASSIGNMENT OF 
SUBCONTRACTS (this “ Assignment”) is made thi s 20th day of March, 2023, by International 
Management Consultants, Inc. d/b/a IMC Construction (“ Construction Manager”), with a mailing 
address at 3 Great Valley Parkway, Suite 200, Malvern, Pennsylvania 19355, to LAKELAND BANK 
(“Lender”), with a mailing address at 250 Oak Ridge Road, Oak Ridge, New Jersey 07438. 
WITNESSETH THAT: 
 Pursuant to a contract dated [_____________] (the “Contract ”) between Construction 
Manager and CSH West Orange, LLC  (“Owner”), Construction Manager has agreed to perform 
certain services, as Lender of the Owner, in connection with Owner’s proposed development of a 
senior housing facility containing 83 units (a 92 -bed assisted living and memory care facility)  on 
a parcel of land containing approximately 3.3 acres l ocated at 609 Eagle Rock Avenue, Township of 
West Orange, Essex County, New Jersey (such construction activities, collectively, the “ Project”).  
According to representations by the Owner, the Lender has agreed to make a loan to Owner in the 
maximum principal amount of $27,577,019 (the “Loan”) pursuant to the terms of a loan and 
security agreement executed or about to be executed by Owner  and Lender (as the same may be 
amended, modified, supplemented or restated from time to time, the “Loan Agr eement”) for the 
purpose of financing Owner’s development and improvement of the Project.  Additional terms are 
defined on Exhibit A, attached hereto and made a part hereof. 
 As represented to Construction Manager by Owner, as security for repayment of the  Loan, 
Owner has executed and delivered or is about to execute and deliver to Lender a General Collateral 
Assignment (the “ General Collateral Assignment”) , pursuant to which Owner assigns to Lender , 
among other things, all of Owner’s right, title and interest in and to the Contract.  As a condition to 
advancing any proceeds of the Loan to Owner, Lender has required that Construction Manager execute 
and deliver this Assignment. 
 NOW THEREFORE, Construction Manager, in consideration of the Lender's agreement to 
extend the Loan to Owner and as security for all of Construction Manager’s obligations under the 
Contract, hereby represents and warrants to and agrees with Lender as follows: 
1. Construction Manager acknowledges Owner has advised Construction Manager that  
Owner has or will execute the General Collateral Assignment in favor of Lender, assigning in favor of 
Lender all of Owner’s right, title and interest in and to the Contract, and Construction Manager hereby 
consents to the same.  In addition, to the extent assignable, upon Construction Manager’s default under 
the Contract, Construction Manager hereby conveys, transfers, assigns and sets over unto Lender all 
of Construction Manager’s right, title, interest and privilege in, to and under any and all contracts and 
agreements, whether now in existence or hereafter entered into as Lender of the Owner: (a) with 
contractors, architects, engineers and other design professionals (including all rights of Construction 
Manager to all plans and specifications prepa red with respect to the Project) and other suppliers of 
services or materials  relating to the construction or operation of the Project; and (b) providing for 
payments to be made to Construction Manager by any person or entity in connection with the Project 
(including, without limitation, all warranties, guaranties and indemnities now or hereafter made by any 
person or entity relating to the Project).
